# Introduction

This page provides an overview of the project, including its purpose and key features.

## Why a Design System?

In a world where users expect seamless interactions and cohesive designs, a design system empowers us to:
- Ensure Consistency: Provide a unified look and feel across all touchpoints.
- Boost Efficiency: Streamline workflows by reducing duplication and enabling reuse of components.
- Enhance Collaboration: Foster alignment between designers, developers, and stakeholders.
- Adapt to Change: Quickly evolve with user needs and technological advancements

## Who Is It For?
- Designers: To craft interfaces that align with our visual language and user experience standards.
- Developers: To build components with robust, reusable code.
- Product Managers: To ensure designs and implementations are consistent with our brand and user goals.
- Anyone involved in creating, maintaining, or evolving our digital products.
